## FINISHING OPTIONS DECISION TABLE

| Finishing | Process | Max Size | Finish Types | Clear Coat? | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|--------------|------------|----------|
| **Raw** | Cut only | Any* | N/A | No | Minimal cost, interior only |
| **UV Printed + Laminate** | Print → Laminate → Cut → Done | 1340×custom | Matte OR Gloss | Optional** | Full color, interior preferred |
| **UV Printed + Clear Coat** | Print → Cut → Clear coat → Done | 1340×custom | Matte OR Gloss | Yes (applied) | Full color, all faces protected |
| **Vinyl Wrapped** | Print vinyl → Laminate → Cut material → Apply | 1340×custom | Matte OR Gloss | No (integrated) | Exterior, max UV durability |

*Paneles sin impresión pueden ser más grandes (custom quote)
**Clear coat = optional add-on to UV Printed + Laminate

## WORKFLOW SELECTOR

**Choose based on your priority:**

### Speed & Cost
→ **Raw** (cut only) or **UV Printed + Laminate**

### Exterior Durability (Australia)
→ **Vinyl Wrapped + Gloss Laminate** (best UV resistance)

### Interior Premium
→ **UV Printed + Laminate** or **UV Printed + Clear Coat**

### Large Format / Custom Size
→ Contact for quote (may require paneling or subcontracting)

## PRINTING SPECIFICATIONS

### Flat Bed UV Printer
- **Maximum width:** 1340mm
- **Length:** Per roll (custom)
- **Color:** Full colour CMYK+
- **Output:** All materials compatible

### Epson Ecosolvente (Vinyl Printing)
- **Maximum width:** 1340mm  
- **Length:** Per roll (custom)
- **Substrate:** White vinyl
- **Color:** Full colour
- **Post-process:** Lamination required (Matte or Gloss)

### Clear Coat Enamel (Manual Application)
- **Thickness:** Super fine protective layer
- **Coverage:** All faces (front, back, edges)
- **Applied after:** Cutting (so edge exposure visible but protected)
- **Optional:** Paired only with UV Printed path

### Laser (Acrylic - In-House & Subcontracted)
- Maximum bed: 1200×2400mm
- Tolerances: ±0.1–0.3mm
- Complex shapes: Often subcontracted
- We handle finishing after subcontract cut

## MOUNTING OPTIONS

| Option | Best For | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Adhesive backing** | Lightweight, smooth surfaces | Indoor primarily |
| **Pre-drilled holes with threads** | All materials, standard install | Most flexible |
| **Standoffs** | Elevation/separation from surface | Modern aesthetics |
| **Screws** | Exterior, high-traffic, heavy materials | Best support for outdoor |

**Selection Rule:**
- Light + small + interior → Adhesive
- Heavy + large + exterior → Screws
- Flexible → Pre-drilled holes

## LOCATION CONSIDERATIONS

### Interior
- ✓ All materials
- ✓ All finishes
- ✓ All mounting options
- No UV restrictions

### Exterior  
- ✗ **PVC NOT recommended** (decolouration)
- ✓ **UV Printed:** Deteriorates over time → Vinyl superior
- ✓ **Vinyl Wrapped:** Best choice for longevity
- ✓ **Back Black ACM:** Ideal for fences (reverse visible)
- **Recommendation:** Vinyl wrapping for max durability

### North-Facing (Australia - High Solar)
- Consider: Vinyl wrapping > UV printed
- Orient: Material selection may change recommendation
- Parameter: Track orientation for quote accuracy

## SUBCONTRACTING

### When We Subcontract
- **Acrylic cutting** (complex shapes or beyond local capacity)
  - We print + laminate → Subcontract cut → We apply vinyl/finishing

### When We Don't
- ACM/Back Black/PVC cutting (in-house CNC)
- All printing (in-house: UV + Epson)
- All laminat ation (in-house)
- All vinyl application (in-house)

### Turnaround Impact
- Subcontracted cuts add 5–10 business days (standard)
- We handle all finishing post-cut

## SALES WORKFLOW (Vendor-Optimized)

**Steps in order of vendor decision-making:**

1. **Shape:** Panel / Custom Shape / Letters
2. **Dimensions:** W × H (mm) - Auto-flags if needs subcontracting
3. **Material:** ACM / Back Black / Acrylic / PVC
4. **Location:** Interior or Exterior?
5. **Finishing:** Raw / UV Print+Laminate / UV Print+Clear Coat / Vinyl Wrap
6. **Finish Type:** Matte or Gloss (if laminate/vinyl)
7. **Mounting:** Adhesive / Pre-drilled / Standoffs / Screws
8. **Notes:** Font details, rush delivery, orientation, substrate notes, etc.

## VENDOR NOTES FIELD - WHAT TO CAPTURE

When entering configuration, capture these details in **Special Notes**:

### Font & Design Details
- Typeface name (if custom font provided)
- Weight (bold, regular, light)
- Spacing/kerning notes
- Any design variations needed

### Logistics & Timing
- "Rush delivery - need by [DATE]"
- "Standard timeline OK"
- "Flexible on deadline"

### Installation Context
- "North-facing wall, high UV"
- "Interior shaded wall"
- "Will be sheltered by overhang"
- "High-traffic area, needs durability"

### Special Requests
- "Touch-up paint colour match"
- "Custom finish texture"
- "Specific mounting angle"
- "Reference image attached (file name)"

### Pricing Notes
- "Budget constraint: $XXX max"
- "Cost-sensitive - recommend cheapest material"
- "Premium budget - quality over cost"

### Production Flags
- "Requires subcontracting - confirm timeline"
- "Complex shape - send proof for approval"
- "Prototype - may need modifications"
- "Batch order - consider discounts"
